KEY EXPERIENCE and STRENGTHS

We have over 40 years of strong exposure and work in the Air Separation industry. Our CEO has been an instrumental player in large Air separation plants built by the top 5 companies in the industry. Our team is conversant using chemical and process design tools from FORTRAN to HYSYS, Aspen, HTRI and so on. We have used these to save our stakeholders a big percentage of costs during project execution.
Our team has worked with diverse projects in the chemical, petrochemical industries, off-shore units and refineries. We have been involved in all stages of a project like but not limited to conceptualization of the project, gathering design system requirements from the client, Basic and detailed design, Construction, Inspection and commissioning. During the whole process we are focussed on safety and use our extensive experience maintaining safety at chemical and Hydrocarbon plants.
We use our expertise to partner with you on fluid mechanics, process control, process design, Heat and mass transfer.
We have worked all across the world and have advised/designed projects in over 10 countries.
